About Dinosaurs

Dinosaurs are a group of reptiles that lived millions of years ago. They first appeared during the Triassic period, over 200 million years ago, and lived until the end of the Cretaceous period, about 65 million years ago. Today, the only descendants of dinosaurs are birds.
